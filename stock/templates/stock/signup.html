About Stock Juaari

Welcome to Religare Online- a share trading web portal that enables trading and investments across all asset classes. The online share trading portal is offered by Religare Securities ltd, one of the leading stock broking companies in India since 1994. Religare Online is powered by simple and smart features designed to cater all your investment needs . It offers valuable and insightful research reports along with advanced self-research tools like Star Ratings and Tech Scan for effective online stock trading. Star Ratings helps you to choose your stock investments based on fundamental company analysis while Tech Scan will aid you to select best performing stocks on the basis of sound technical analysis. Moreover, the platform has been designed to sense what you need and personalize what you see. Our real time stock trading platform is power packed with useful features like advanced portfolio tracker, live market watch, stock screener, technical analysis of stocks and many more. “Dynami” - Religare Online’s mobile trading app gives you freedom to trade anytime anywhere across all mobile operating systems as well as tablets. Religare Online also offers an exclusive research enabled trading platform exclusively for derivatives customers. Open an online trading account with Religare Securities today and enjoy these exclusive features for free!
